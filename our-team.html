Meet Our Team

We are a passionate group (of one) of developers and designers dedicated to creating the Memory of Me experience. Here's our(my) story:

Once upon a time in the mystical decade known as the 90s, our lone adventurer, a hero armed only with a mouse and a questionable internet connection, stumbled into the magical world of point-and-click games. Little did they know that those countless hours spent solving pixelated puzzles and navigating quirky characters would plant the seed of a dream: "One day, I shall create my own story!" Fast forward through the years, and this brave soul embarked on the ultimate quest: learning how to code... alone. Armed with caffeine, determination, and a collection of browser tabs that would make anyone dizzy, they fought their way through countless tutorials, late-night coding sessions, and the occasional existential crisis ("What even is a semicolon?!").
